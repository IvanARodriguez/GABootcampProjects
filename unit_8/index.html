Platforms we work!

Adobe Audition

Mixing and mastering

We combine the contents of multiple clips in the same time range, creating a single clip that you can quickly edit in either the Multitrack or Waveform Editor.

Adobe Audition was the first program we started to use on it's earlier version 1.5, we have kept updated so we can develop a better sound to our customers using more features on Adobe Audition CS6.

Logic Pro X

For Quality and Efficiency

Logic Pro X provides us with a variety of tools that facilitate the the quality of sound for your idea from that initial melody in your head to the complete resulting piece of music.

Today, Logic Pro X has more plug-ins, loops, and instruments than ever for a complete music production suite. We usually use Logic Pro to master the final project after making sure the tracks are on time on Adonbe Audition.

Fruity Loops

The Beat Machine

Many professional producers of famous artists such as Jay-Z, Kanye West and Drake, use this platform to create hip-hop, trap, dancehall, electronic music, among others.

We as a recording studio also make sure we are up to date with this instrumental production software. We create sounds and customize them in fruity loops to have more development.
